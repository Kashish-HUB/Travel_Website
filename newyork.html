Cultural Insights

New York City is a cultural melting pot with influences from around the world. It's known for its iconic festivals like the Macy’s Thanksgiving Parade and New Year’s Eve at Times Square.

Travel Tips for New York

  • Currency: US Dollar (USD)
  • Local Etiquette: Tipping is expected (15-20%).
  • Weather: Summers are hot, winters can be snowy.
