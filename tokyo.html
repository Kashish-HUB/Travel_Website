Cultural Insights

Tokyo blends ancient traditions with modern innovations. Experience the elegance of tea ceremonies, visit ancient temples like Senso-ji, and immerse yourself in the buzz of Shibuya Crossing. Don’t miss seasonal festivals like the Cherry Blossom Festival in spring and the Sumida River Fireworks in summer.

Travel Tips for Tokyo

  • Currency: Japanese Yen (JPY)
  • Local Etiquette: Bow when greeting, avoid talking on phones in public transport, and use both hands when giving or receiving things.
  • Weather: Spring (March-May) is ideal for cherry blossoms, and autumn (September-November) offers colorful foliage.
